BACKGROUND INFO

Itzy, stylized as ITZY, is a South Korean girl group under JYP Entertainment that debuted on February 12, 2019. The members are Yeji, Lia, Chaeryeong, Ryujin, and Yuna. The group recently released their first studio album "Crazy In Love" with its lead single "LOCO" on September 24, 2021. The album features 16 tracks, 6 of which are instrumental tracks to previous releases. "Sooo LUCKY" and "LOCO" show a side of ITZY that MIDZYs have not seen before: the cool girl group has a soft side. ITZY's previous tracks "Not Shy" and "WANNABE" are cool-girl songs about being independent and true to yourself.
